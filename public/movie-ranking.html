My Top 3 Movies of all Time :)

Soul

I like this movie because of the Jazz music and it is a very nice Pixar movie. Great teachings about life.

Monster's Inc.

Another great Pixar movie. great laughs and a good family movie.

Great cinematics. Really good to watch if you have read all the books, so you can compare the novel to the movie. Highly encourage to read the books beforehand.
